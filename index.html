Epic The Musical

Overview:

EPIC: The Musical is a group of concept albums of a loose adaptation of Homer's Odyssey that was created by Jorge Rivera-Herrans. He wrote the music and lyrics, sang as Odyseus, orchestrated, and produced the tracks. His song clips and process videos have gone viral on social media with over 60 million views across TikTok, of over 80,000 people who share memes, fan theories, cover songs, positive affirmations, and tattoos of the lyrics.

In EPIC: The Musical, there are 2 acts with 9 different sagas divided among them:

Act 1 Has five sagas: The Troy Saga, The Cyclops Saga, The Ocean Saga, The Circe Saga, and The Underworld Saga.

Act 2 Has four sagas: The Thunder Saga, The Wisdom Saga, The Vengeance Saga, and The Ithaca Saga.

My Favorite Song - Warrior of the Mind

Background Information:

Warrior Of The Mind is the fifth and final song of The Troy Saga where Athena reminds Odysseus that he must close off his heart and follow her teachings as he is the 'Warrior of The Mind'. The song also has a flashback to the past when Odysseus and Athena first met and she became his mentor.
